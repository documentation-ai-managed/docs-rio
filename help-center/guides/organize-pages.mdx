## Overview

Rio helps you keep documentation tidy with folders and page order.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a folder">
    ````
    Click "New Folder" in your project sidebar to group related pages.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Move pages">
    ````
    Drag and drop any page into a folder to organize.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set page order">
    ````
    Drag pages up or down in the list to reorder them as needed.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
